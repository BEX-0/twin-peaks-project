About the Show

The series follows an investigation, headed by FBI Special Agent Dale Cooper and local Sheriff, into the murder of homecoming queen Laura Palmer in the fictional town of Twin Peaks, Washington.

The show's narrative draws on elements of detective fiction, but its uncanny tone, supernatural elements, and campy, melodramatic portrayal of eccentric characters also draws from American soap opera and horror tropes. Like much of Lynch's work, it is distinguished by surrealism, offbeat humor, and distinctive cinematography.
